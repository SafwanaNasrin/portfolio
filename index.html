About Me.

I’m Safwana Nasrin, a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast and ethical hacker dedicated to making digital environments safer. I specialize in web application security, penetration testing, and threat detection, with hands-on experience building tools for phishing detection, vulnerability scanning, and SIEM-based monitoring systems. My work combines technical expertise with a keen eye for detail, allowing me to identify security gaps and develop practical solutions that protect organizations from cyber threats.

I enjoy exploring emerging technologies in cybersecurity, contributing to open-source projects, and continuously learning new techniques to stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of security. Beyond technology, I love solving complex problems, participating in CTF challenges, and sharing knowledge with the cybersecurity community. My goal is to transform my skills into impactful solutions, helping businesses and individuals navigate the digital world safely and securely.
